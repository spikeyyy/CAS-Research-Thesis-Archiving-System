EARIST Manila, also known as the Eulogio "Amang" Rodriguez Institute of Science and Technology, is a prestigious educational institution located in Manila, Philippines. The university was named after Senator Eulogio "Amang" Rodriguez, a renowned Filipino educator and statesman. EARIST Manila is committed to providing quality education and promoting academic excellence in various fields of study. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs across different disciplines, including engineering, technology, education, business administration, computer science, and more. With a rich history spanning several decades, EARIST Manila has established itself as a leading institution in the field of science and technology education. It aims to produce highly skilled professionals who can contribute to the development and progress of the nation.
